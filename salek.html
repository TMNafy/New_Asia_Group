ABOUT SALEK TEXTILE LTD

SALEK TEXTILE LTD (ROTOR UNIT) STARTED ITS COMMERCIAL OPERATION FROM APRIL, 2009. IT IS A SUBSIDIARY COMPANY OF MALEK SPINNING MILLS LTD. AND PRODUCES OPEN-END YARN, WHICH IS PRIMARILY SUPPLIED TO LOCAL DENIM FABRIC AND GARMENTS MANUFACTURERS.
