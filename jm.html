ABOUT JM Fabrics

JM FABRIC LTD WAS ACQUIRED BY THE NEW ASIA GROUP IN 2011. IT SPECIALIZES IN MANUFACTURING LADIES’ LINGERIE, MEN’S INTIMATES, AND CUT & SEW JERSEY INCLUDING SEAMLESS FUNCTIONAL SPORT’S WEAR. IT ACCUMULATES WITH A FULL TRACEABILITY FROM RM TO FINISHED PRODUCT FOR A DIVERSIFIED PRODUCT RANGES. AS OUR OPERATIONS ARE 100% VERTICAL, WHICH MEANS WE START FROM SPINNING ALL THE WAY TO FINISHED GARMENTS, INCLUDING PRINTING AND EMBROIDERY AND VARIOUS EMBELLISHMENTS. WE ARE ALREADY A KEY SUPPLIER TO SOME OF THE LARGEST NAMES IN RETAIL ON THE HIGH STREET SUCH AS NEXT, PAUL SMITH, GUESS, INDITEX, H&M, AMERICAN EAGLE AND EDDIE BAUER. IN ADDITION, WE ARE A KEY SUPPLIER FOR A UP AND GYMWEAR BRAND IN THE UK. WE ARE THE ONLY FACTORY IN BANGLADESH PRODUCING HIGH QUALITY PERFORMANCE ACTIVE WEAR IN SANTONI SEAMLESS FOR MULTIFARIOUS SPORT’S CUSTOMER.
