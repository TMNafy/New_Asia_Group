ABOUT NEW ASIA

The New Asia Group is comprised of six individual entities that form a vertically intergrated and appareal manufacturing organization, headquarteded in Dhaka, Banglades.

Establised in 1987, the Group started out with Rahim Textile Mills, a dyeing and printing mill for both knit and woven fabrics,which supply some of the world's most well-recognized brands

Since then, they have expanded into the production of greige yarn ( for both knitted and denim garments and sweater for both knitted and denim garments and sweater garments

The New Asia Group is one Bangladesh's leading private organizations and is poised for further growth under the guidance of its dynamic leadership team and keep this to another level.
