ABOUT NALEK SPINNING MILLS LTD

MALEK SPINNING MILLS LTD (“MSML”) IS ONE OF THE LARGEST AND MOST MODERN SPINNING MILLS IN BANGLADESH WITH NEARLY 64,000 SPINDLES. ITS FOUNDERS ARE A GROUP OF DYNAMIC ENTREPRENEURS WHO HAVE MADE AN IMMENSE CONTRIBUTION TO DEVELOPMENT OF THE TEXTILE SECTOR OF BANGLADESH. THE COMPANY BEGAN ITS COMMERCIAL OPERATIONS IN JANUARY 1991 AND WAS SUBSEQUENTLY CONVERTED INTO A PUBLIC LIMITED COMPANY ON SEPTEMBER 2008. MSML WAS ONE OF THE FIRST USTER-CERTIFIED SPINNING MILLS IN BANGLADESH AND PROCURES ITS COTTON FROM THE FINEST FARMS IN THE USA, WHICH ENABLES IT TO PRODUCE A CONSISTENTLY SUPERIOR QUALITY OF YARN TO THOSE OF ITS PEERS. MSML IS A DEEMED EXPORTER AND 100% OF ITS YARN IS SUPPLIED TO LOCAL (KNIT) GARMENTS MANUFACTURERS, WHO THEN EXPORT THESE GARMENTS TO WORLD-RENOWNED APPAREL RETAILERS.
